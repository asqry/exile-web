Moderation

Exile has a wide range of Moderation commands to manage your community with ease. Featuring report commands, that save to a database, your reports will stay secure.

Exile has an advanced ticket system that allows users to get support easier than ever before! Just type !ticket and see the options!

Welcome & Leave Messages

Whenever a new user joins your server, Exile will welcome them so you can rest easy knowing your new members are happy and feel welcome. When a user leaves your server, Exile will mention that too, it's a bit awkward though... No hard feelings.

Exile will make sure that your moderators know exactly what's going on behind the closed doors of your server's bedroom. Exile's logging feature is like a parent that always makes you leave your door open when friends are over.
